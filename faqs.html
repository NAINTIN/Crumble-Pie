FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

HOW DO I PLACE AN ORDER?
You can place your order through our Facebook page or Instagram account at the Crumble Pie.
We accept orders from Monday to Wednesday each week.
DO YOU DELIVER?
Yes. We have our own in-house rider for cheaper delivery rates. You may also book third-party
couriers such as Lalamove, Grab, or MrSpeedy to pick-up your confirmed order and deliver it to you.
WHEN ARE THE DELIVERY DAYS?
All confirmed orders are delivered every Friday.
DO YOU OFFER FREE DELIVERY?
We offer free delivery in selected areas in Upper Antipolo.
For other locations, an additional fee will apply depending on the distance.
DO YOU ACCEPT SAME-DAY ORDERS?
No, we do not accept same-day orders. All items are baked freshly on a pre-order basis to ensure
the highest quality. Please place your order between Monday and Wednesday for Friday delivery.
DO YOU ALLOW PICK-UP/WALK-INS?
No, we we operate exclusively through pre-orders. Since the business is home-based, so we prepare pies
per batch to maintain quality and manage time properly.
WHAT IS THE SHELF LIFE OF YOUR PIES?
Our pies can last 1–2 days at room temperature and 3–4 days when refrigerated. For best taste
and texture, we recommend reheating them in an oven or air fryer before serving.
HOW MANY SERVINGS PER BOX?
Each pie is good for 6–8 servings depending on how it is sliced.
WHAT PAYMENT METHODS DO YOU ACCEPT?
We accept GCash, Bank Transfer, and Cash on Delivery(COD).
DO YOU ACCEPT BULK ORDERS?
Yes, we accept bulk orders! Please place your bulk order at least once a week in advance to ensure we
can accomodate your request.
